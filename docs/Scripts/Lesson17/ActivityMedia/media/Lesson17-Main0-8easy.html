What will happen to workers if their wages stay the same?
Keep It the Same
Written by Dr. Walker, Department of Economics, University of Antarctica
There has been considerable debate about whether the government should raise minimum wage in America.I present to you here a few reasons as to why the government should not raise minimum wage.If employers raised minimum wage, this would in turn increase the cost of products we buy and ultimately increase our federal debt. With our inflation rate as it is, many common items are already almost out of reach for millions of Americans.Additionally, those who are currently employed may not want to lose their job just to help someone receive a raise. If this increase takes place, 500,000 more Americans will find themselves unemployed in this economy. 1 Currently, low wage workers have an easier time finding work without the additional competition. 2 If the wage were to increase, it would be harder to find jobs because the number of unemployed unskilled workers would be so high. 3 Is it worth getting 16.5 million people out of poverty or leaving 500,000 workers unemployed? I think its not worth it at all.
